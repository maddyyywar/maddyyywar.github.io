Acadia National Park

Acadia National Park is located on Maine's Desert Island and is around 47,000 acres big. It is one of the most beautiful areas in Maine. This National Park is home to the Cadillac Mountain, where the first U.S sunrise occurs every morning.

Hiking Tips:

  • Use caution near steep cliffs.
  • Always hike with more than one person
  • Take all necessities with you
  • Tell someone where you going before you embark
  • Always be aware of your surroundings
