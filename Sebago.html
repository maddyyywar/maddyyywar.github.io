Sebago Lake

One of the prettiest lakes in the Northeast!

Sebago lake is the deepest lake in Maine with its deepest point being at 316 feet in some areas. It is also 12 miles long. Alot of people visit for the serene atmosphere but it also attracts water sport enthusiast. Tourists ride jetskis, tube on boats and parasail.

What can you do:
  • Sea Coast Adventure
  • Sebago Lake Rentals
  • Point Sebago
