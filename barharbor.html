Bar Harbor

Bar Harbor is a pristine town on Maines northern coastline. It is small with a population of 5,394 people as of 2016. People visit to explore the towns quaint shops and restaurants as well as to enjoy the views of Acadia National Park. Beaches line the Atlantic Ocean with views of islands such as Cranberry Island and Porcuine Islands.
